How Android Pipeline Works

1. Desktop Chrome fetches tabs from your Mobile every strictly 12 hours.
2. Found URLs are piped safely to localhost.
3. Panop Backend determines if URL matches "Articles" or "Books" based on your Rules.
4. Bookmarks are updated in panop_output/.
5. RIS citations are dynamically appended into batches automatically organized by ISO Week (e.g., articles_week_43.ris).

Routing & Categories

Article Domains

Routed to "Android Articles" bookmark folder. Appended to articles_week_XX.ris

Book Domains

Routed to "Android Books" bookmark folder. Appended to books_week_XX.ris
